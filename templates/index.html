Welcome to the Linkifyr Tunnel Service

This service allows you to expose your local server to the internet securely. Explore the features, see a demo, and download the client application to get started.

Features

Our service offers the following features:

  • Dynamic urls and no login required
  • Securely exposes local servers to the internet.
  • Handles HTTP/HTTPS requests and forwards them to the local server.
  • Displays connection status and information using a user-friendly CLI.
  • Simple setup and easy to use.
